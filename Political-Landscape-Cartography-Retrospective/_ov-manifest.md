# Political-Landscape-Cartography — Retrospective Design Analysis

> **This cartridge analyzes Political-Landscape-Cartography v1.0.0 ([github.com/JawnLam/Political-Landscape-Cartography](https://github.com/JawnLam/Political-Landscape-Cartography) — private) as if it had been designed using OVE v2.1's protocol. PLC-Retrospective demonstrates Q6b (three-layer mastery signal) concretely.

## What this OV is

Political-Landscape-Cartography (PLC) is an operating volume for mapping and navigating organizational political landscapes. The operator (a consultant or a principal-self-coaching) opens a sphere cartridge per engagement, populates it with actors / resources / cycles, and runs ADAPT Loop cycles to make decisions under political constraint. The substrate is the Methodology Author's 2018 Pepperdine dissertation on political capital, the OPC Assessment v3.0 sphere diagnostic, the ADAPT Loop coaching framework, and the Political Warfare Catalogue (782 tactics across 16 strategies, 84 clusters, 50 theorists).

Key features:

- **Sphere cartridges** — one per engagement, each holding actor map, resource ledger, cycle log, decision audit trail
- **9-type resource taxonomy** (information, attributes, position, relationships, credibility, time, materials, expertise, mandate)
- **ADAPT Loop** — five-beat cycle protocol (Agenda / Decide / Act / Perceive / Track) for political-decision discipline
- **OPC Assessment v3.0** — 17-section sphere diagnostic with 6-level hierarchical IDs
- **72 pattern atoms** across 6 families (stakeholder archetypes, change-initiator postures, opposition postures, arc dynamics, power-dynamics themes, ethical dilemmas)
- **Three operator modes** — consultant, principal-self-coach, principal-with-multiple-spheres
- **Restrictive LICENSE** with 14-day evaluation window, self-coaching exclusion, Academic Archive Carve-Out
- **Methodology Author's dissertation ships by reference** (Convention 9) — physical PDF held local; placeholder `.md` directs readers to contact author

## Why this user designed it

The Methodology Author is a management consultant focused on organizational development, workflow automation, AI integrations, and enterprise politics. The 2018 Pepperdine dissertation on political capital was an academic codification of a decades-long body of working method. The consulting practice required an operationalized, AI-loadable form of that method — substrate-agnostic, multi-session, and disciplined enough to use during live client engagements. PLC is that operationalization.

## Who is this for

Private release (restrictive LICENSE; ships by reference, IP-attorney review pending). Designed for two operator modes:

- **Consultant mode** — the Methodology Author and authorized peer practitioners using PLC in paid engagements
- **Principal-self-coach mode** — operators authorized by the Methodology Author to coach themselves in their own political navigation (typically senior executives the Author works with directly)

Unauthorized evaluation is governed by § 1 of the LICENSE (14-day window with 6 limits; application-prohibited).

## Cadence

- **Consultant mode:** sphere-open at engagement-start; cycles run weekly to monthly during the engagement; sphere-close at engagement-end. Multiple spheres in parallel across the consultant's portfolio.
- **Principal-self-coach mode:** sphere-open at major role transition; cycles run on principal's own cadence (often monthly to quarterly); sphere-close at next major role transition. Typically one or two spheres at a time.

## Multi-session evidence

Overwhelming. Political capital accumulates over years; spheres span months to years; cycles span weeks to months. ADAPT's *track* beat IS the multi-session contract — last cycle's perceived-vs-actual outcomes inform next cycle's framing.

## Output target

**Per cycle:** audit-trail-integrity log (the L1 mastery signal). **Per engagement:** sphere-close retrospective (the L2 retrospective deliverable — closest PLC has to a terminal artifact). **Per operator practice:** longitudinal signals exported at sphere-close — forecast-skill calibration, no-loop discipline rate, pattern-tagging accuracy, recursive resource accumulation, cross-sphere portfolio quality (L3).

This three-layer specification is the canonical Q6b answer that v2.0 codified. PLC was the substrate that produced the spec.

## Notes for future practice-archetype OV designers

PLC is the first practice-archetype OV shipped via OVE. Its key innovations that should generalize to future practice OVs:

1. **The sphere / engagement / practice three-layer cartridge model.** Spheres are per-engagement (closeable); the practice is per-operator (open-ended). Both layers track signals; only the engagement layer ships terminal-class artifacts. This pattern likely fits longevity-health OVs (per-protocol-cycle / per-quarter-review / per-decade-practice), leadership-development OVs (per-engagement / per-role / per-career), and financial-stewardship OVs (per-decision / per-quarter / per-lifetime).
2. **The 9-type resource taxonomy.** Practice OVs benefit from explicit resource enumeration because resources are the substrate of accumulation. PLC's taxonomy is dissertation-grounded; other practice OVs should derive their own from their domain's substrate, not copy PLC's.
3. **The audit-trail-integrity L1 signal.** "Did the cycle's discipline complete?" as a per-cycle yes/no is the cleanest mastery signal for practice domains where outcomes are noisy and trajectory is long.
4. **The sphere-close retrospective as the L2 closest-to-terminal artifact.** Bounded, concrete, observable — and recurring on every engagement-close.
5. **Convention 9 ship-by-reference for sensitive substrate.** Practice OVs often build on the Methodology Author's personal academic or proprietary work. The convention lets the methodology ship publicly without redistributing the substrate.
